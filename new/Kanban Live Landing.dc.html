How it works

Scan it in, scan it out. The board does the thinking.

Install Kanban Live on a barcode scanner, RFID reader or an iPad and scan everything as it moves. You get an instant, always-current snapshot of stock — wherever you are in the world.

01

Scan stock as it moves

Every part in and out is a scan. No spreadsheets, no manual counts, no clunky data entry to slow the line down.

02

Bins signal in real time

Each bin reports its own status. When stock drops past its trigger, the card flips to red and a demand is raised — automatically.

03

Reorder exactly on time

Kanban Live links to your MRP/ERP to order new stock as, and only when, you need it — fewer hold-ups, happier customers.

Leaner inventory

No complex functions, no useless reports. Just what you need to know about current stock in real time — and clear reports that flag your problem areas.

Easy on any device

A simple, intuitive app for iPad, iPhone and the web that controls your inventory and speeds up processes. It's your shop floor's best friend.

Flexible reports

Act fast on new trends and see the bigger picture over time. Expect right-sized bins, lower costs, and the inventory levels you actually need.
