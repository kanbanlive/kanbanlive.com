Easy set-up

Changing the software your business relies on every day seems like a big step. But with Kanban Live it's easy — we'll organise the data transfer while you carry on doing what you do best. Everything's secured in the cloud, ready for you to start maximising your inventory.

Simple to use

When you're leading change, it's hard to get buy-in on the shop floor — especially when your vision involves new systems. Give the team something easier to use and it's a different story. Kanban Live's iPad and iPhone apps mean a shallow learning curve and a logical, familiar control system that takes no time to master.

Efficient integration

Kanban Live integrates with Socket Mobile and Linea Pro barcode scanners — all your team does is scan stock in and out, and the system takes care of the rest. Automatic reports go to your MRP/ERP to reorder stock, keeping you just-in-time and as lean as possible. You can also run a twin-bin system, perfect for high-volume, low-cost parts.

Always available

Wherever you have a Wi-Fi signal you have instant access to your inventory from phone, tablet or computer. No more wasted trips to the shop floor for out-of-stock items, and an accurate number you can quote in a meeting. It doesn't matter where you are in the world — Kanban Live is always on, 24/7.

Scalable growth

Worrying about the size of your inventory? Need the option to scale up or down when the time is right? Kanban Live offers flexible plans for any size of business. And because we don't insist on contracts, we can grow with you or shrink with you — whichever, whenever is best for you.

No user limits

Businesses have different needs, and it isn't fair to impose user limits. We charge only for the number of bins you use — so you can stay as flexible as you like and bring your whole team on board at no extra cost.
