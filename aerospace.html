Welcome to Kanban Live!

Kanban Live is an inventory management system underpinned by the kanban principle of stock control. It's a simple and efficient way of serving your direct line feed (DLF) clients and getting the best out of your shop floor.

Who Can Use It?

Kanban Live was designed for the aerospace supply chain industry, and tackles the challenges it faces to achieve continuity of production. If you need the right part available, in the right place and at the right time, then Kanban Live can help.

How Does It Work?

Stock control doesn't get any simpler. We'll set it up to work with your current system and you just use the app with a barcode scanner or RFID reader. Kanban Live will give you an instant snapshot of your stock levels at any given point in time, wherever you are in the world.

Streamlined Inventory

Streamlining your stock and simplifying your processes are the first steps towards maximising your profitability. Kanban Live gives everything you need to know about your current stock in real-time, and identifies your problem areas so you can minimise waste.

Simple Interface

You can use Kanban Live with Apple's iPad, iPhone, and iPod. It's an easy-to-learn app that works perfectly with a two bin system and helps the shop floor work quicker and more accurately. It can be tough to get buy-in from the troops when changing systems, but Kanban Live makes it easy. Try our free demo and let them play with it – we're confident they will love it.

Accurate Ordering

Production held up because you can't supply the parts? Not any more. Kanban Live syncs with your MRP/ERP systems, allowing for fully automated ordering. Say goodbye to staff squirrelling fasteners away - and to those severe penalties for late delivery.

Flexible Reporting

Kanban Live gives you a complete overview of what you need and what you are wasting. If you need to lower your business costs and identify your high-profit inventory, then it's all there for you to read in simple and effective reports.

Boost Your Productivity

No more pointless trips to the shop floor to find stock. Kanban Live's access-from-anywhere feature means you can view what you have while out on the road, in the office or even at home.

Reduced Human Error

Scan products accurately in and out of your inventory, with human error kept to absolute minimum. Kanban Live also integrates with Socket Mobile and Linea Pro barcode scanners to make those all-important counts on the shop floor faster.
